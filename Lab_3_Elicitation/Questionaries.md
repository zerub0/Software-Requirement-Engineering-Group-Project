# Questionnaire / Survey
## Bus Ticketing System

## 2.2 Questionnaire/Survey Questions

**Purpose:** To collect feedback from many people efficiently and identify common patterns.

---

### Section 1: Demographic Information

| **No** | **Question** | **Response Options** |
|--------|--------------|---------------------|
| Q1 | How often do you travel by bus? | ☐ Daily<br>☐ Weekly<br>☐ Monthly<br>☐ Rarely |
| Q2 | What is your age range? | ☐ 18-25<br>☐ 26-35<br>☐ 36-50<br>☐ 50+ |
| Q3 | Do you own a smartphone? | ☐ Yes<br>☐ No |

---

### Section 2: Current Ticketing Experience

| **No** | **Question** | **Response Options** |
|--------|--------------|---------------------|
| Q4 | How satisfied are you with the current manual ticketing process? | ☐ Very Satisfied<br>☐ Satisfied<br>☐ Neutral<br>☐ Dissatisfied<br>☐ Very Dissatisfied |
| Q5 | What is the average time you spend at the terminal to purchase a ticket? | ☐ Less than 10 minutes<br>☐ 10-30 minutes<br>☐ 30-60 minutes<br>☐ More than 1 hour |
| Q6 | Have you ever missed a bus due to tickets being sold out? | ☐ Yes<br>☐ No |

### Section 3: Desired Features

| **No** | **Question** | **Response Options** |
|--------|--------------|---------------------|
| Q7 | How important is online booking to you? | ☐ Very Important<br>☐ Important<br>☐ Neutral<br>☐ Not Important |
| Q8 | How important is seat selection to you? | ☐ Very Important<br>☐ Important<br>☐ Neutral<br>☐ Not Important |
| Q9 | Would you use a mobile app for bus ticketing? | ☐ Yes<br>☐ No |

---

## Questionnaire Summary

| **Section** | **Questions** | **Purpose** |
|-------------|---------------|-------------|
| Section 1: Demographic Information | Q1 - Q3 | Understand respondent background |
| Section 2: Current Ticketing Experience | Q4 - Q6 | Identify pain points with current process |
| Section 3: Desired Features | Q7 - Q10 | Gather feature and payment preferences |

---

## How to Distribute the Questionnaire

| **Method** | **Description** |
|------------|-----------------|
| **Google Forms** | Create an online form and share the link via email, WhatsApp, or social media |
| **Paper Survey** | Print copies and distribute at bus terminals or among classmates |
| **Email** | Send the questionnaire directly to respondents |
| **In-Person** | Conduct face-to-face surveys with passengers at bus terminals |
