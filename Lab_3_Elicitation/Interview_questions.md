# Interview Questions
## Bus Ticketing System

---

### Stakeholder Interview Questions

| **Stakeholder** | **Interview Question** |
|-----------------|------------------------|
| **Bus Operator** | What are the biggest challenges you face with the current manual ticketing system, what features would you like to see in the new system to make your job easier, and how do you currently manage bus schedules, routes, pricing, and seat allocations? |
| **Bus Company Owner** | What are your main business goals for implementing this system, how do you currently track revenue, what are your budget and timeline expectations, and what would you consider a successful implementation? |
| **Transport Authority** | What regulations must the bus ticketing system comply with, how should the system ensure fare transparency and safety standards, what data should the system report to your office, and what is your approval process for new bus routes? |
| **Passenger** | How do you currently book bus tickets, what frustrations do you experience with the current process, what features would make the booking process easier, and how would you prefer to make payments, receive notifications, and access the system? |
| **Bus Driver** | What information do you currently receive about passengers before departure, what would help you confirm passenger boarding more efficiently, what ticketing issues have you encountered, and what features would help you report problems quickly? |
| **Customer Support Staff** | What are the most common passenger complaints or issues you handle regarding ticketing, how do you currently process refunds, and what features would help you assist passengers more efficiently and reduce complaints? |

### Interview Guidelines

| **Guideline** | **Description** |
|---------------|-----------------|
| **Duration** | 10-15 minutes per interview |
| **Format** | Face-to-face or virtual (Zoom/Google Meet) |
| **Recording** | Take detailed notes during the interview |
| **Follow-up** | Ask clarifying questions if responses are unclear |
| **Tone** | Professional, friendly, and open |
