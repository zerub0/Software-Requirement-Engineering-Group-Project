# Brainstorming Session
## Bus Ticketing System

---

## 2.3 Brainstorming Session

**Purpose:** To generate a wide range of ideas, features, and requirements for the system.

---

### Session Details

| **Item** | **Details** |
|----------|-------------|
| **Session Title** | Bus Ticketing System - Feature Brainstorming |
| **Duration** | 60-90 minutes |

---

### Participants

- Bus Operator (1-2)
- Bus Company Owner (1)
- System Developer (1-2)
- System Tester (1)
- Passenger Representatives (2-3)
- Customer Support Staff (1)

---

### Session Agenda

| **Time** | **Activity** | **Description** |
|----------|--------------|-----------------|
| 0-5 min | Introduction | Explain the purpose of the session |
| 5-10 min | Ground Rules | No criticism, encourage wild ideas, build on others' ideas |
| 10-40 min | Idea Generation | Participants suggest features and ideas for the system |
| 40-60 min | Categorization | Group similar ideas together |
| 60-75 min | Prioritization | Vote on the most important ideas |
| 75-90 min | Summary | Summarize key findings and next steps |
